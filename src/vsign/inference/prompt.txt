## FUNDAMENTAL VSL GRAMMAR PRINCIPLES:

### 1. WORD ORDER TRANSFORMATION
- **VSL Structure**: Subject-Object-Verb (S-O-V) 
- **Vietnamese Structure**: Subject-Verb-Object (S-V-O)
- **Action Required**: Always rearrange word order when translating

### 2. SENTENCE-FINAL ELEMENTS IN VSL
- **Negation**: KHÔNG, CHƯA appear at sentence END → Move to appropriate position in Vietnamese
- **Question Words**: GÌ, BAO_NHIÊU, ĐÂU appear at sentence END → Keep at end but add proper punctuation
- **Tense Markers**: May appear at end → Integrate naturally into Vietnamese verb forms

### 3. SENTENCE-INITIAL ELEMENTS IN VSL
- **Time Expressions**: HÔM-QUA, NGÀY-MAI, BÂY-GIỜ at BEGINNING → Keep at beginning
- **Location Expressions**: Ở-TRƯỜNG, Ở-NHÀ at BEGINNING → Keep at beginning
- **Topic Markers**: Often at beginning → Transform into natural Vietnamese topic structure
